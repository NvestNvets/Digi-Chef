Latina Guapa Sofrito Recipe

A traditional Puerto Rican cooking base

🌿 What is Sofrito?

Sofrito is the flavorful foundation of many Puerto Rican, Dominican, and Caribbean dishes. It combines garlic, onions, peppers, herbs, and sometimes tomatoes to build a rich base for stews, rice, beans, and meats. It's passed down from abuelas and mamas through generations.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 head of garlic (peeled)
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 bunch of culantro (if available)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Optional: 2 plum tomatoes

👩🏽‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Rinse all herbs and veggies.
  2. Chop large items into chunks.
  3. Add all ingredients to a blender or food processor.
  4. Blend until chunky-purée consistency.
  5. Store in mason jars or freeze in portions for later.
