Sofrito is the flavorful foundation of many Puerto Rican, Dominican, and Caribbean dishes. It combines garlic, onions, peppers, herbs, and sometimes tomatoes to build a rich base for stews, rice, beans, and meats. It's passed down from abuelas and mamas through generations.
